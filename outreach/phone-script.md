# Phone Follow-Up Script

Use this 5 days after sending the email if no response.

---

**Opening:**
"Hi, is [Contact Name] available? ... Hi [Name], this is Patrick Hannon — I'm a web designer here in the Oak Forest area. I sent you an email a few days ago about [Business Name] — I actually built a quick demo website for you to look at. Did you happen to see it?"

**If yes:**
"Great — what did you think? ... [Listen] ... I can customize it however you'd like, and I can have the real version live within a week. Do you have a few minutes to talk about it?"

**If no / didn't see it:**
"No problem — I can resend it right now while we're talking. Basically I noticed you didn't have a website, so I built one for free just to show you what it could look like. There's no obligation — I just find it's easier to show than tell. Can I resend that to [email]?"

**If not interested:**
"I totally understand. Can I ask — is it mainly a budget thing, or just not a priority right now? ... [If budget] I do have flexible payment options if that ever changes. [If not a priority] Completely fair. Mind if I check back in a few months?"

**Closing (if interested):**
"Great. What I'd suggest is a quick 15-minute call to go over what you want on the site — services, hours, photos, whatever. Would [day] or [day] work for you?"

---
*Tips:*
- Call mid-morning (9–11am) or early afternoon (1–3pm) — avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons
- Be conversational, not salesy — you're a neighbor who built them something
- Always mention you're local / Oak Forest area
