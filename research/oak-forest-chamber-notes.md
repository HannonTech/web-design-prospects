# Oak Forest Chamber Research Notes

**Source:** Oak Forest-Crestwood Chamber of Commerce — oc-chamber.org/member-directory
**Researched:** 2026-06-09

## Method
Reviewed chamber member directory listings. Any member with no website URL listed,
or whose URL resolves to a Facebook page, GoDaddy placeholder, or aggregator page,
was flagged as a prospect.

## Key Findings
- Several established businesses (10–30 years old) have zero web presence beyond Facebook
- Construction and trades are the weakest segment for web presence
- Bars/restaurants often rely on Yelp + Facebook only
- Gmail addresses in chamber listings = strong signal of informal/weak digital setup
